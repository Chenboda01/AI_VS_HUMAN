How to Play

  • Each player (Human and AI) gets 10 turns.
  • On your turn, choose one action: Answer Questions (gain points), Send Troops (attack opponent), or Defend House (reduce damage).
  • AI uses a multi-objective algorithm to balance between answering questions, sending troops, and defending.
  • After 20 turns total, the player with the highest score wins!

Goal: Outscore your opponent by making strategic decisions each turn.
